Why Tinkl?

Tinkl introduces coding concepts through visual puzzles without the frustration of typing syntax. We focus on core computational thinking:

  • Ages 4-6: Sequencing and step-by-step logic.
  • Ages 6-8: Pattern recognition and loops.
  • Ages 8-12: Conditionals (If/Else) and abstract functions.
